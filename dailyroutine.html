Daily Routine

My daily routine is the most essential part of the process. It is combined action with habit. Consistent, deliberate action towards the goals I will accomplish and the vision I have for my present and future. It is not thinking about external factors I can't control, it is working on what I can change and knowing that it will lead me exactly where I want.

Time is fleeting. That's why it's important to focus on things that invest in time, not spend it. Eliminating distractions: researching random purchases, reddit, NF. These are things that I should have a time and place for, not allowed to rule my life nor ever get in the way of what I really want.
